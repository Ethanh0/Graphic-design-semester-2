About Us

Did you know 50% of food wasted by supermarkets are still fresh and safe to eat? Here at Scraps we are proud to take tasty fruit and veg that would usually be thrown away by supermarkets and turn them into healthy nutritious salads for everyone to enjoy! We work on a pay as you feel basis so come down and find out about the new food craze everyone is talking about! Scraps! fights the battle against food waste in the most ethical, sustainable and mouth-watering way!
